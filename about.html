About David

David Pomeranz

We assist in everything from divorces to legal separations, from custody to support. Much of what happens in family court is confusing, and the Court uses a lot of jargon. We help decipher what you need to know and zealously advocate for your rights. What you don't know is often more important than what you do know!

Our office has 25 years' experience in handling the toughest matters, including move-aways, Dept. of Child Support Services hearings, property and debt division, parental alienation and paternity cases.
